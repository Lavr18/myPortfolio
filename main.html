Hello, my name is Aliaksei Laurynovich and I study MSc Internet of Things at University of Essex. I recently graduated from the University of Edinburgh with 2:1 degree in BEng Electronics and Electrical Engineering with Honours. During my time in Edinburgh I worked on various systems - audio and video amplifiers, simple games on FPGA boards, low-powered devices to communicate via LoRa network, microcontrollers for remotely-controlled cars, Python GUI and web-applications. Furthermore, for the last two years I was one of developers of Phenotiki platform, which is an affordable system for image-based plant research. I improved its sensor communication system, web-interface and GUI. I completed my final year BEng Project under the supervision of one of Phenotiki founders, Dr Sotirios A Tsaftaris.
